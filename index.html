Race Strategy

RACE.

Goal
Finish healthy and strong.
Sub-4h is realistic. Sub-3:45 if everything clicks. Do not target time — target execution.

Run-walk 9:1 from km 1 to km 42. Set a Garmin interval timer. When it beeps, walk. Do not negotiate with yourself at km 10 because you "feel good" — those are the athletes limping at km 32.
Go / No-Go Gate
Apr 25 long run (25–35 km) — the body's vote.
Criteria to START the marathon:
✓ Hamstring: zero pain during + zero morning-after soreness
✓ Tendons: no sharp point pain
✓ HR: able to maintain run-walk at Z2 through 25km+
✓ Completed 25km+ without pain-forced stops
✗ Any fail above → scratch marathon, no debate.
Season pivot: half marathons + triathlon build.

Race Nutrition
6–7 gels during race. More than you think you need. Practice 2–3 on long runs in Weeks 3–4. High-sodium drinks are not for carb loading — water only or low-sodium sports drink.
Race Rules
Carb Loading (–3 to –1 days)
Lower-fiber sources only. No whole grains, no raw veg, no high-sodium drinks.
–3 days (Thu)
350–400g carbs/day, normal sources. Glycogen top-up starts.
–2 days (Fri)
450–500g carbs, lower fiber. White rice, pasta, bread, banana, juice.
–1 day (Sat)
500–550g carbs, very low fiber. White rice, bagels + jam, banana, sports drink. Zero vegetables, zero whole grains.
Race morning
100g oats + 30g protein + banana + coffee. Same as half marathon — familiar, tested.
Recovery Stack
Morning (non-negotiable): 500ml water before anything. Magnesium glycinate on waking if not taken before bed. Collagen + Vit C 45–60 min before training.
Post-training: 40g protein + fast carbs within 30 min. Massage gun: quads, hamstring belly only, calves, glutes — 90 sec each. Flector on any tendon hot spots.
Sauna: Post-session, 15–20 min at 80–90°C. 500ml water before, 500ml + pinch of salt after. 4–5× per week minimum. Race week: max 12 min, 70°C, once only.
Massage gun — hamstring rule: Never on the insertion point behind the knee. Belly and glutes only. Low speed on sensitive areas.
